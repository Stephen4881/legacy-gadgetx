Build a premium, trust-first eCommerce website called “Legacy Gadgets”.

Core Identity:
Legacy Gadgets is a modern tech accessory brand focused on quality, durability, and long-term value — not cheap, disposable gadgets. The brand should feel established, confident, and curated.

Design Style:
- Dark, luxury-inspired theme
- Minimalist layout with generous spacing
- Clean typography (modern sans-serif)
- No flashy colors or animations
- High contrast for readability
- Premium, Apple-like aesthetic

Homepage Structure:
1. Hero Section
   - Strong headline: “Accessories Built to Last.”
   - Subheadline: “Curated tech accessories for iPhone, gaming, and sound — chosen for quality, performance, and everyday reliability.”
   - Primary CTA: “Shop Collection”
   - Secondary CTA: “Explore iPhone Accessories”

2. Trust & Value Section
   Display short, confident trust signals:
   - Quality-Checked Products
   - Secure Payments
   - Reliable Delivery
   - Customer Support That Responds

3. Featured Categories
   Create clean category cards for:
   - iPhone Accessories
   - Audio & EarPods
   - Speakers
   - Gaming Accessories
   - Power & Charging

4. Best Sellers Section
   - Show 4–6 curated products
   - Clean product cards with image, name, and price
   - No clutter or unnecessary badges

5. Brand Value Section (“Why Legacy Gadgets”)
   Explain clearly:
   - Products are curated, not mass-listed
   - Focus on durability, performance, and design
   - Accessories chosen to match modern devices and lifestyles
   - A brand built for trust and long-term use

6. Footer
   Include:
   - About Legacy Gadgets
   - Contact
   - FAQs
   - Shipping & Returns
   - Privacy Policy
   - Terms of Service

Product Page Structure:
- Large, high-quality product images
- Clear, confident product name (avoid generic model numbers)
- Short paragraph describing the outcome and experience of using the product
- Bullet points focused on benefits, not technical specs
- Clear pricing and “Add to Cart” button
- Trust reminders (secure checkout, easy returns, support)

Tone & Language:
- Professional, calm, confident
- Human and trustworthy
- Avoid hype, slang, or exaggerated claims
- Write as a brand that expects to be trusted

Technical Requirements:
- Mobile-first responsive design (iPhone priority)
- Fast loading and lightweight
- Clean semantic HTML structure
- SEO-friendly headings and metadata
- Simple, intuitive navigation

Important Rules:
- Do NOT overcrowd the store with products
- Do NOT use low-quality placeholder text
- Do NOT design like a generic dropshipping store
- The store must feel like a real brand with standards

Goal:
Create an eCommerce website that makes visitors feel safe, confident, and assured — a store that looks and feels like it has been around for years, even if it is new.
